## 6. Predictive & Social Growth Strategies

### Social Network Growth (Predictive)
- **Interest Clustering:** Automatically detect users who comment on the same 3+ assets/blog categories and suggest they "Follow" each other. This creates "Community Silos" that increase time-on-site.
- **Micro-Influencer Detection:** Notify Level 1-2 users when their post gets Level 5 attention. *Trigger: "A top expert just liked your analysis!"* This significantly boosts the 'Contribution Loop'.
- **Trending Social Velocity:** Identify comments receiving reactions at a rate of >10 per minute and push to relevant asset-trackers.

### Behavioral Prediction
- **Churn Prevention (The 'Re-Hook'):** Detect a drop in session frequency (e.g., from daily to weekly). System triggers a high-value "Market Recap" personalized to their most viewed assets exactly 24h after their last predicted session time.
- **Engagement Optimization:** Use historical data to send notifications at the user's most active hour (Personalized Delivery Window).
- **Sentiment Shift Alert:** If a user consistently posts Bullish sentiment but the market/expert-consensus shifts Bearish, send a "Risk Assessment" notification.

## 8. Implementation Roadmap & Notes

### Scalability & Delivery
- **Frequency Capping:** Implement a global limit (e.g., max 5 push/day) to prevent user fatigue.
- **Exclusion of SMS:** All notifications are strictly digital (Web/App/Email) to minimize costs and focus on the "Second Screen" experience.
- **Personalization Engine:** Notifications should use the user's `username` or `name` in the payload for higher engagement.

### Future Expansion
- **Sentiment-Based Pushes:** Only notify users of news/analysis that matches their historical sentiment (Bullish/Bearish).
- **Geofenced Market News:** (Future capability) Alerts based on local physical market hours or regional events.
